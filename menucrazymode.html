About me
Ever curious, creative and interested in the world of the arts, I decided to begin my university career in the field of design. First, I attended a Bachelor’s Degree in Interior Design at Politecnico di Milano, during which I was also able to spend a semester in Paris at École Penninghen, further enriching my cultural background. Once back in Milan, I continued my studies in Communication Design to have a broader view on so many aspects, such as research and graphic design skills. In fact, I believe that design is a powerful medium of expression with endless declinations, and for this reason, with each design project I undertake, I embrace the opportunity to explore and evolve my creative capabilities, recognizing that there is always room for innovation and growth. This is why now I am currently facing a new experience abroad, at Aalto University, in Finland.
I am determined to push the boundaries of what is possible, hoping to remain in constant evolution.
