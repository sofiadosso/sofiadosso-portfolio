Mutevole

Editorial design

2023

Mutevole is a magazine that embraces the reflective essence related to climate change, exploring the topic from an introspective point of view. Through a special focus on personal stories, one is immersed in the experiences of individuals living and directly confronting environmental matters. Issue 00 is entitled Frozen in time and the protagonist is ice, a substance that has always determined a strong connection with the Earth and the landscape and that becomes a repository of knowledge.

Contents are mainly divided into three article and photo sections: Melting Landscapes, Resculpting Mores, Shattered Recollections. In them, respectively, there are testimonies related to the theme of thawing glaciers, cultural practices and visual imagery. All of them are preceded by an introduction always related to the main topic. And to conclude, there are as well an insert with an interview with an expert on glacier landscapes and an illustrated story.

A transmedia system also accompanies the magazine: this is a web platform through which people can submit their own stories or testimonies related to climate change in a specific place in the world. These are archived within the site and then they are available for consultation by anyone interested in discovering more about them.
